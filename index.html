Section B (VSA)
[2] 17. State Henry’s Law and give its one application.
Show Answer
Law: Solubility of a gas in a liquid is proportional to the partial pressure of the gas above the liquid. Application: Solubility of CO₂ in soft drinks/soda.
[2] 18. Differentiate between Molecularity and Order of reaction. (Any 2 points)
Show Answer
1. Order is experimental, Molecularity is theoretical. 2. Order can be zero/fractional, Molecularity is always a whole number.

(a) What is the oxidation state of Fe in Hemoglobin?
(b) Name the ligand in Hemoglobin.
(c) Why are coordination compounds often colored?

Section E (Long Answer)
[5] 31. (a) State Raoult's Law for a solution containing non-volatile solute.
(b) 18g of Glucose (C6H12O6) is dissolved in 1kg of water. At what temperature will the water boil? (Kb = 0.52 K kg mol-1)
Show Answer
(a) Relative lowering of VP is equal to mole fraction of solute. (b) ΔTb = Kb * m. m = (18/180) / 1 = 0.1. ΔTb = 0.52 * 0.1 = 0.052. Boiling point = 373.15 + 0.052 = 373.202 K.
